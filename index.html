    About Me

    I’m a UX designer drawn to work that centers people, care, and real-world impact. I enjoy collaborating closely with others to make sense of complexity and design experiences that feel thoughtful, inclusive, and grounded in real needs.

    In recent years, I’ve focused on social impact work, contributing to projects that support well-being and access through research-driven design and early-stage problem solving.

    Outside of design, I enjoy dance, yoga, making art, and spending time with my family — practices that help me stay present and grounded.
